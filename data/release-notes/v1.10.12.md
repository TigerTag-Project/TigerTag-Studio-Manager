### Added

- **Notification center.** A new bell in the sidebar, with an unread badge, gathers your incoming friend requests and updates in one place. Friend requests shown there are actionable — Accept / Decline / Block — and stay until you choose one, so a request is never lost. First update type: "X accepted your friend request."
- **Friend requests are easier to handle.** Pending requests now also appear in the Friends panel and the notification center, not just the popup, each showing the requester's real avatar with Accept / Decline / Block.

### Changed

- **Friends list updates live.** Accepting or removing a friend now appears instantly for both people without reopening the panel, and a friend's avatar, name or colour changes show up live too.
- The Friends button now shows your number of friends (or a "+" when you have none) instead of the pending-request count; requests moved to the bell.
- The friend-request popup gained a close button to dismiss it for later (the request stays pending and can be handled from the bell or Friends panel), and it now shows the requester's real avatar immediately instead of an initials flash.
