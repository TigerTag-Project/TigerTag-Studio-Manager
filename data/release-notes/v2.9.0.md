# Tiger Studio Manager v2.9.0

Wishlists that shop like a real cart — quantities, running totals, and everything grouped by store — plus offline QR codes.

### Added

- **Quantities on wishlist items.** Set how many of each spool you want, just like an online cart — pick a number from the list or type your own for larger amounts. Friends see the quantities on your shared lists too.
- **A payment summary on every list.** Each list now shows a running total — subtotal, estimated tax and grand total — in your own currency and price mode, right next to it.
- **The "To order" cart is sorted by store.** Everything you need to buy is grouped by where it comes from (one section per shop, with a count), and anything without a buy link sits in its own section at the end — so you can order store by store.
- **QR codes now work fully offline.** Every QR in the app — your public list, friend invites, the mobile-app code — is generated on your machine, no internet needed and nothing sent to an outside service.
- **Pick your QR colours.** Your shareable QR codes can be styled from a set of colour presets, and your choice sticks on that device.
- **Download your list's QR code** as a high-quality image, straight from the public-link card, ready to print or share.
- **Tell us you have a TigerPOD.** A quick toggle in the TigerPOD window lets you say you own one — and we tick it for you automatically the first time you scan a tag.

### Changed

- **A friendlier version history in "What's New."** The little dots at the bottom now glide as a carousel with the current version always centred, so you can flip back through past updates without losing your place.
- **Switching sections clears your filters.** Moving between Inventory, Favorites, Lists and Printers now resets the search and filters that belonged to the section you left, so you always start fresh.
- **The "Public link" card also appears on a friend's public list**, so you can grab and share their list's QR, link or download too.
- **Invite links now use tigersystem.io**, our public web address for shared pages.
- **The heart button is now an "Add to cart" button.** Same idea — it drops the spool into your "To order" cart — with a clearer cart icon and colour.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a false "low stock" alert could appear at launch, before your inventory had finished loading.
- Fixed an issue where your friend or follower count didn't update on the spot when someone added you.
