# Tiger Studio Manager v2.11.1

Clearer buttons, plus fixes for twin-tag spools and a friend's stock value.

## Improved

- The "add to your To-order list" button now has its own cart-with-a-plus icon, so it's no longer mistaken for the "buy at the shop" button sitting next to it.

## Fixed

- Fixed an issue where scanning a spool with two linked NFC tags could briefly open the wrong card, showing blank/reset details instead of the spool's own picture and shelf. Existing two-tag spools are repaired automatically.
- Fixed a friend's total stock value showing far too low — it now counts their whole shelf, not only the items you also own.
