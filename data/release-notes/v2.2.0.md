## v2.2.0 — 2026-07-09

A follow-up to the Products & Favorites release: see where a favorite came from, tidy up the product card, and set prices on several filaments at once from anywhere.

### Added

- **See where a favorite came from.** When you star a friend's filament it's saved to your favorites with an "Added from" card showing their photo and name — kept up to date automatically, and still shown even if you're no longer friends. Tap it to jump to their inventory when you still can.
- **Set a price on several filaments at once from the Inventory.** Tick a few spools and apply one price — you no longer have to be in the Products table to do it.
- **Sort the Favorites table.** Click any column header — Brand, Material, Name, Stock, Minimum, To order or Price — to sort it up or down.

### Changed

- **A clearer product card.** Bigger filament picture, the colour name on its own line so it's never cut off, and the favorite/like buttons moved up next to the close button.
- **"+ Material" button.** The old "Create a TigerTag Cloud" button is now simply "+ Material", with a little info tip explaining it adds a new spool of that exact filament — no RFID chip required.
- **Low-stock reminders open your shopping list.** Tapping a "running low" notification now takes you straight to the "To order" list instead of a single filament.

### Fixed

- Fixed a renamed **rack** not refreshing in the Storage view until you left and came back.
- Fixed the **grouped filament view** opening behind a printer's side panel instead of beside it.
