## Security

- Fixed a flaw where a link stored on a filament — a buy link or an attachment shared by a friend — could carry a dangerous, non-web address that ran when clicked. Every link is now checked, and only real web addresses (http/https) open.
- Hardened how the app opens external links and how it fetches product images, so a page or image address coming from someone else's data can no longer reach anything on your machine or your local network.

## Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a YubiKey (or similar security key) was mistaken for an RFID reader when plugged in, because it presents a smart-card interface to the system. Security keys are now ignored.
- Fixed a leak where switching accounts or signing out left Snapmaker, FlashForge and Creality printers still connected in the background.
- Fixed an issue where the "NFC Data" preview in Add Product was empty until you changed a field.
- Fixed the alignment of the step badges in the container "How to measure" guide when a step's caption ran onto two lines.
- Made low-stock checks lighter, so large inventories update more smoothly.

## Changed

- Length-limited fields now give a quick shake when they're full, so you can tell your keystroke was rejected instead of silently ignored.
- The **Stock value** now shows whether it's excluding or including tax right next to the figure.
- Chipless spools created in Tiger Studio now use a cleaner **TigerData** identifier, and the "NFC Data" preview label reads **TigerData** to match.
- The rack **Subtitle** field is now labelled **Description**, and list names and occasions are capped at a tidy length.
