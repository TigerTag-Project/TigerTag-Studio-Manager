### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the Filaments Grid and Table views flashed whenever a single spool changed. Moving a weight slider, picking a container, linking a twin or changing a colour rebuilt every card or row; now only the spool that changed is touched, and the rest keep their product images intact.
- Fixed an issue where the printer grid's per-printer job block (state, progress bar, filename) rebuilt on every status poll even when nothing changed; it now updates only when the state, progress, remaining time or filename actually changes.
