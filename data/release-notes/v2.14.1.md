## Fixed

- Fixed spool thumbnails being accidentally "picked up" and dragged around in the Inventory (grid and list) and Favorites views, where dragging isn't meant to do anything.
- In the rack view, moving a spool from one rack to another no longer lights up the whole panel while you drag — it stays calm, and only the slot you're about to drop into is highlighted.
- On Windows, the import window no longer pops up by mistake while you drag a spool around in the grid or rack view.

## Changed

- The **minimum-stock** button now looks and behaves like the "Add a buy link" and "Add a price" buttons — one tidy button to set it, with the value and an edit pencil once it's set.
