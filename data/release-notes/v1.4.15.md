### Added

- **Creality: live integration.** Connect to a Creality printer over your local network and monitor it in real time: nozzle, bed and enclosure temperatures, print state, a job progress bar, layer counter and estimated duration. The CFS multi-colour box shows each slot's assigned colour and material, and models that support it stream an inline WebRTC camera and the current print thumbnail. An online / offline badge reflects the connection state.
