## New

- **Export and import your filaments as `.ttag` files.** Back up a spool — or a whole selection — to a file you can keep, move to a USB stick, or send to someone else, then bring it back in anywhere. It works for TigerData, TigerTag and TigerTag+ spools alike, keeps twin spools together, and shows a full preview before anything lands in your inventory. On import you choose how it comes in: **Restore** puts everything back exactly as it was, or **Import** drops the materials in as fresh spools you own. You can pick and choose which materials to bring in, and pull several files in at once — by browsing, by pasting a link, or by dragging files onto the window.
- **Weigh spools with a USB scale.** Plug in a Dymo USB scale and your spool weights fill themselves in. With a reader connected, just set the spool on the scale and its weight is saved automatically; with a spool's card open, a quick confirm does it. The live reading appears right in the spool's weight panel.

## Fixed

- Fixed an issue where you couldn't scroll the **To order** card far enough to reach the "Add material" button and the fields below it.
- Fixed the **Select** button not showing when the app first opened — it only appeared after switching views.
- On Windows, you can now scroll to reach a rack lower down the list while dragging a spool.
- Taking a spool out of a rack no longer lights up the whole drop zone in orange while you drag — it stays calm.
- Opening a grouped stack of spools now toggles cleanly — click again to close it — and in multi-select the checkbox does the selecting while a click on the card opens the stack.

## Changed

- The **Add a price** button in the To order card now matches the "Add a buy link" button, and shows your account's currency symbol.
- A filament you add by hand now starts as a **full spool (100 %)**.
- Chipless spools now read as **TigerData** throughout, matching the identifier introduced last release.
