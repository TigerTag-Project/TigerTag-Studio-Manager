### Added

- **Duplicate a spool (×N).** A new Duplicate tool at the top of the spool toolbox (hold to confirm) mints one or many copies in a single action, with a −N+ quantity stepper (1–50). Available for TigerCloud and basic TigerTag spools (TigerTag+ can't be duplicated). Copies carry no twin link and no rack placement, and are never auto-paired as twins.
- **Editable spool note.** The spool's name is now an inline-editable free-text note: click the name in the detail panel, type, and press Enter to save. Available on every spool type — on TigerTag+ the catalogue name stays read-only with the editable note below it. There's a 28-character budget with a thin usage bar, and editing the note flags the spool (and its twin) for a chip re-write.

### Changed

- **The third tier is now called TigerCloud** everywhere (badge, stat tile, filters), renamed from "TigerTag Cloud".
- Added the Bambu Lab X2D model, and camera transport (JPEG vs RTSP) is now driven by the printer model catalogue for more robust behaviour across the lineup.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a Bambu Lab printer's IP did not show in the printers table; it now appears and sorts correctly.
