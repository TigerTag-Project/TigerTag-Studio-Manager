### Changed

- **Spool and printer side-cards no longer dim the screen.** Opening a spool's or printer's detail card used to drop a dark overlay behind it that you had to dismiss first. The list now stays fully usable — click another spool or printer and the card switches in place. Each card has a clear orange close tab on its left edge that slides in and out attached to the card.
- **Spool and printer cards can now sit side by side.** Open a spool's card and a printer's card at the same time and the printer keeps the right edge while the spool card tucks in just to its left, instead of one replacing the other.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where some buttons — most visibly the show/hide-password eye — shifted downward when pressed.
- Fixed an issue where, with both a spool card and a printer card open, closing the spool card briefly swept its close tab in front of the printer card instead of behind it.
