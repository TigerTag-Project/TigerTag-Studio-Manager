### Added

- **Creality: live machine controls.** The Creality side panel can now drive the printer, not just monitor it. Tap a temperature to set the nozzle, bed or chamber target; jog X/Y/Z and home the axes; disable the motors; and control the part-cooling, case and side fans with 1%-precision sliders. The motion pad is hidden during an active print so a stray move can't disturb a job; temperatures stay adjustable. Tested on a real K2 Plus.
- **Creality: filament slot selection and CFS load/unload.** Click a filament slot to select it as the active filament; it's highlighted, and Feed and Unload buttons appear that load or unload that slot through the CFS (the printer auto-heats, cuts and feeds). Slots now clearly show three states — identified (coloured), loaded-but-unidentified, and empty — and RFID-identified slots are locked from editing, with editing moved to a dedicated pencil button.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where starting a print and deleting on-printer files from the app silently failed on Creality K-series printers; both now reach the printer correctly.
