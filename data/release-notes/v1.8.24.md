### Changed

- **The app now opens already populated.** On launch, your inventory, friends and avatar appear in the very first frame from the previous session's local cache; live data from the server then merges on top silently, repainting only what changed. Product thumbnails are served from a local cache too, so they no longer re-download on every launch.
- **New launch splash screen.** A small TigerTag splash with the logo and app version shows the instant you open the app, and the main window only appears once it's ready to display fully.
- Your sidebar friends list now scrolls when you have more friends than fit on screen, while the avatar, buttons and community footer stay fixed in place.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where your avatar and your friends' avatars flashed or reloaded several times on every cold start; they now paint once and stay put.
- Fixed an issue where a shimmer animation swept across every image while it loaded; images now simply appear.
- Fixed an issue where your initials could show behind or beside your avatar photo, or the "+" sign-in badge could leak next to your initials. The avatar now always shows exactly one of: the "+" (signed out), your initials (no photo), or your photo.
