### Added

- **Guided encode from TigerCloud to a physical chip.** Encoding a TigerCloud spool now opens a guided flow instead of a one-shot click. The burn stays locked until every connected reader holds a chip, with each reader's state shown live and a single progress bar. Chips are written one after another and each write is read back and verified byte-for-byte, so a chip only turns green on a confirmed match. Nothing is committed to your inventory until every chip verifies — any failure, including a chip moved off the reader mid-write, cancels the whole sequence with nothing written. It warns before overwriting a non-blank chip, refuses two identical chips, and writes a twin pair identically.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a freshly-burned chip reported a wrong "Manufactured" date (around 2056); the date is now correct.
