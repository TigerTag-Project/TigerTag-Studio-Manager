### Added

- **Invite QR code.** The Friends panel's "My code" card now shows a QR of your shareable invite link, so a friend can scan it to add you.
- A friendly nudge (plus a notification) to add a profile avatar if you haven't set one; both disappear the moment you do.
- **Update download progress.** The auto-update toast now shows a filling progress bar with a rough time-left estimate while a new version downloads.

### Changed

- **Reworked account menu.** It now shows you at the top, with a hover Switch account fly-out listing your connected accounts, plus Edit profile, Friends and Settings. Manage accounts opens a redesigned modal where each account has a Switch button and a menu to disconnect.
- **Friends panel refresh.** The friends list moved to its own companion card that opens beside the panel, each friend row has a clear chevron to view their inventory, and removing a friend now asks for confirmation.
- **Cleaner sidebar.** The avatar and friend chips keep the same size whether the sidebar is collapsed or expanded, stray separator lines are gone, and the community buttons are simplified to GitHub / 3D Files / Discord.
- Scrollbars are hidden across the Materials and Printers grids and tables, the "My printers" header is gone, and the empty-printers screen now invites you to add your first printer.
- **Live "ready to scan" indicator.** A small pulsing green dot shows when a TigerPod reader or a TigerScale is connected; scanning is automatic, so the old "+ Auto Scan" button is gone.
- Edit your avatar from the header by hovering it next to your name.
- Toolbar "+" button labels shortened to Material / Device.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where scanning a spool while viewing a friend did not return you to your own inventory; it now does and opens the scanned spool's card.
- Fixed an issue where some coloured buttons turned invisible (white-on-white) on hover.
- Fixed an issue where Auto-organize could move a spool out of its slot when switching between accounts before racks had finished loading.
- Fixed an issue where the blocked-users list did not refresh when opening the Friends panel.
