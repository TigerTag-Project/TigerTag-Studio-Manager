### Added

- **Always-visible chip actions.** A spool's chip actions (write, restore, erase, recycle) now always appear in its toolbox: enabled when the matching chip is on the reader, greyed out otherwise. With no reader connected, clicking a greyed action explains what a TigerTag Player would let you do. For a twin-tagged spool (two chips), these actions enable only when both chips are on readers, since they act on the pair.

### Changed

- The tag type (TigerTag+, TigerTag, TigerCloud) now appears as a badge at the top of a spool's Details instead of being repeated as plain text lower down.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue on Windows where RFID readers (ACR122U / TigerPOD) connected while the app was already running were not detected; they are now picked up automatically within a few seconds, without restarting the app.
- Fixed an issue where the notifications panel overlapped a spool, printer, Friends, Settings or other side panel; it now steps aside when one opens.
- Fixed an issue where scanning only one chip of a twin-tagged pair broke the pairing between the two chips.
- Fixed an issue where placing or lifting a chip while a spool was open caused the material video to flash or restart.
- Fixed an issue where scanning an already-open spool opened a second card on top of it.
