### Added

- **Open the connection tutorial straight from printer settings.** The configure panel for a Bambu Lab, FlashForge or Elegoo printer now has a "Connection tutorial" button at the top, for when you skipped the tutorial during the scan and still need the access code or LAN-only setting. The tutorial follows whichever model you have selected in the dropdown.
- Scan results now show the printer's product photo, making it easier to recognise your models at a glance.

### Changed

- **One shared "extra subnets" list across every brand, synced to your account.** The power-user "other networks to scan" list now appears for Bambu Lab too, and is the same list everywhere. It is saved to your account so it follows you across devices, and existing entries from the old per-brand lists are merged automatically.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a FlashForge printer on a different subnet than your computer (a typical multi-VLAN home network) was not found by the LAN scan; it is now discovered the same way the mobile app does.
- Fixed an issue where the connection tutorial in printer settings always opened the same tutorial regardless of the selected model, most visible on Bambu Lab.
