### Changed

- **Cold start is now instant from cache.** The app stores your latest inventory, racks and printers locally, so on the next launch they appear immediately — even before the network round-trip completes — and the app stays usable when offline. Only the actual changes since your last session hit the network.
- Product thumbnails no longer flash on view switches; clicking Grid, opening the detail panel, or any background update no longer makes every thumbnail blink.
