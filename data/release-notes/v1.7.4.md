### Changed

- **Deleting a spool is now permanent.** Spools are fully removed rather than kept as hidden "deleted" entries, and a deleted spool can no longer be resurrected by the mobile app reconnecting. Any leftover hidden entries from older versions are cleaned up automatically. The debug "Show deleted" view has been removed.
- Spools now get a container assigned automatically. A spool without a container is matched to the right one from its brand on the next sync, so newly added spools no longer sit without a container.
