### Added

- **See whose inventory you're viewing at a glance.** A bright bar on the left edge slides to the avatar of the view you're currently in — your own or a friend's — so you always know where you are.
- Distinct sounds when you switch: a quick blip between your own accounts, and a softer one when you peek into a friend's inventory.

### Changed

- Friendlier wording across several empty states and confirmations.
- **Clearer inventory table.** The remaining-weight bar now sits under the value with more room, grouped rows highlight properly on hover again, and the sort arrow is a cleaner chevron.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where clicking the friend you were already viewing bounced you back to your own inventory.
- Fixed an issue where scanning a two-chip (twin) spool opened its card twice.
- Fixed an issue where grouped rows did not sort by their combined weight and capacity, so the order did not match what you saw.
