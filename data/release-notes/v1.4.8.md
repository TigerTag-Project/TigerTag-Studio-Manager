### Added

- **Snapmaker network discovery.** A scan panel slides in from the right and finds Snapmaker printers on your local network, adding a printer in one click with its WebSocket already connecting. There's also an "Add by IP" option with live validation, and saving an IP change reconnects automatically.
- **Manual twin-pair repair.** When a spool isn't paired but a matching candidate exists, a repair tool in the spool toolbox links them together. Candidates are matched strictly on brand, material, type, tag version and exact colour.
- **Spool toolbox in the detail panel.** A tidy set of actions — scan colour, scan TD, link/unlink twin, remove from rack, delete — with a hold-to-confirm animation for destructive actions.

### Changed

- **Rack management improvements.** Drag a spool outside any rack to send it back to the unranked panel, with a matching eject animation. Empty spools show in the unranked panel but are excluded from every count, and a per-spool "Remove from rack" action (hold to confirm) sits in the toolbox.
- A friend's filament edit sheet is now clearly read-only, matching the editable layout with its controls disabled.
