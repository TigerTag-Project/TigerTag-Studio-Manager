# Tiger Studio Manager v2.6.0

Notifications become a lasting feed, the inventory table lets you add a price in one click, and the "To order" button now shows how many spools are waiting in your cart.

### Added

- **Add a price right from the inventory table.** Filaments without a price now show an "Add price" link in the Price column — click it to jump straight into the price editor.
- **Cart counter.** The "To order" button now carries a small red badge with the number of filaments waiting in your cart, always up to date.

### Changed

- **Notifications now stick around.** Low-stock alerts no longer disappear on their own — they're saved to your account, synced across your devices, timestamped, and kept in your history. Opening the panel marks everything read (there's also a "Mark all read" button), the list keeps your 40 most recent, and notifications can no longer be deleted.

### Fixed

- Fixed the "Buy me a coffee" notification: it now shows the proper cup and can actually be clicked to open the support page.
