### Added

- **Shareable friend links.** The Friends panel has a new "Share link" button that copies a link you can send anywhere. When a friend opens it on their computer, Tiger Studio Manager comes to the front with the Add-friend search already filled in — they just press Send request. A link can never add or accept anyone on its own; you always confirm. If they don't have the app yet, the page offers a download; on a phone it shows the code to add manually.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where friend avatars appeared only after restarting the app. The add-friend search preview and a friend you just accepted now show their real profile photo immediately.
- Fixed an issue where accepting, declining or blocking a friend request, or dismissing a notification, lingered for a second instead of clearing the moment you clicked.
