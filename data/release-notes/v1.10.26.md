### Added

- **Add Anycubic cloud printers without leaving the app.** Signing in to your Anycubic account now happens right inside the add-printer panel, with no separate window. Once signed in, your cloud printers appear ready to add.
- A "welcome back" sound when you return from a friend's inventory to your own.

### Changed

- **Calmer, more consistent "Add a printer" flow.** Pick a brand and its options dock beside the list instead of replacing it; every brand now looks and behaves the same, with a tidy header carrying a Back button and a connection tutorial. Adding a printer opens it straight away.
- Anycubic: "Add from Anycubic Cloud" is now the recommended, top option when adding an Anycubic printer.
- **Sidebar footer tidy-up.** The Mobile Apps QR code sits above the GitHub / 3D Files / Discord buttons, the block stays pinned to the bottom, and everything keeps its size and place smoothly whether the sidebar is open or collapsed.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the Anycubic Files button floated on its own instead of sitting next to the light in the controls, like the other printers.
- Fixed an issue where the sidebar version number, community buttons, notifications bell and avatar changed size or position when the sidebar was toggled.
- Fixed an issue where a working button showed a spinner next to a leftover icon; it now shows just the spinner.
- Fixed an issue where opening a friend's inventory left a previous spool or group card from a different view still open.
