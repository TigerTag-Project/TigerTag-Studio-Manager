# Tiger Studio Manager v2.11.0

A sharper "To order" shopping experience, wishlists that reach into your friends' shelves, and smoother loading everywhere.

## New

- The "To order" cart now splits into one card per shop, each with its own running subtotal (tax-included or tax-excluded, matching your setting).
- Reorder those shop cards by dragging them into the order that suits your shopping run.
- Add a personal note to any shop card — "also grab 3 nozzles and a bed cleaner" — and it stays with that card.
- Any item in your reorder list can now be dropped for good with a press-and-hold "Stop tracking" button.
- Favorites, To-order, Lists and Storage now show a loading animation while they fetch, instead of briefly flashing an empty screen.

## Improved

- From a friend's spools, you can now favorite, add to your cart, or drop an item straight into one of your own lists — it's copied over with its picture, price and buy link.
- The grouped-spools panel gained quick favorite / to-order / add-to-list buttons, right on the shelf.
- A product's buy button now sits neatly under its price, matching the look of the reorder panel.
- The loading animation is a playful comet with a rotating set of one-liners to keep you company.

## Fixed

- Fixed the right-click menu (Cut / Copy / Paste / Select All) showing the wrong language when your app and system languages differ.
- Fixed the "Lists" counter sometimes showing a friend's number after you returned to your own view.
- Fixed the decorative storage rack staying on screen after leaving a friend's empty Storage view.
- Fixed the mobile-apps QR code flashing a broken image for a moment when the app started.
