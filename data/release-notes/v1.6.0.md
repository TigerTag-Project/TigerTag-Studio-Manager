### Added

- **Elegoo: full live integration.** Connect to an Elegoo printer over your local network (UDP discovery auto-detects them, with manual IP entry as a fallback) and monitor it in real time: an active job card (filename, progress bar and percentage, estimated remaining time, layer counter, thumbnail and state), nozzle / bed / chamber temperatures with heating indicators, and a filament card for both single-extruder and the 4-slot Canvas hub. A control card gives you a jog pad, Z and home controls, step and print-speed selectors, live position, an LED toggle and a folder button, plus per-fan cards (Model / Aux / Case) with ±10% steppers. A files sheet lists print history and printer-side files, and a filament edit sheet lets you set a slot's colour, material and vendor. Localised across all nine languages.
- **Bambu Lab: live integration.** Connect to a Bambu Lab printer over your local network (LAN mode, authenticated once with the printer's access code) and see a live job card, nozzle / bed / chamber temperatures, the AMS filament slots with humidity and temperature, a camera feed, and an online badge in the printer grid and side panel.
