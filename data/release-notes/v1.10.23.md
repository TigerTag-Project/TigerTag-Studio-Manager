### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where switching between two signed-in accounts could knock spools out of their racks. After placing spools in a rack on one account, jumping to another account and back could make Auto-organize treat those spools as "not stored" and reassign them to the wrong slots. The app now fully resets the previous account's racks on every switch, so your storage layout stays exactly where you put it.
