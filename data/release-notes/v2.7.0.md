# Tiger Studio Manager v2.7.0

Wishlists arrive: build lists of filaments, share them with friends or with a public link anyone can open — QR code and social buttons included. Reordering anything (lists, friends, printers, cameras, racks) now feels smooth, with the other items gliding out of the way.

### Added

- **Lists (wishlists).** Create as many named lists as you like — for a birthday, Christmas, or just a restock plan. Add filaments to a list from any material or product, add a price and a shopping link, and browse each list as rows or a grid. Every list can carry a short message for whoever's looking.
- **Share a list.** Each list can be **private** (just you), **shared with friends** (they see it live), or **public**. A public list gets its own web page anyone can open — no account needed — with a QR code, a one-tap "Copy link", and share buttons for Facebook, X, LinkedIn, WhatsApp and email.
- **Friends see your lists.** Your friends can browse the lists you share with them, right inside the app.
- **Public accounts add friends automatically.** If your profile is public, incoming friend requests are accepted instantly — no need to be online or tap anything. You get a notification telling you who just connected.
- **Drag to reorder friends.** Rearrange your friends in the left sidebar or the Friends panel, and the order sticks across your devices.
- **Right-click to copy and paste.** Any text field now has a proper right-click menu with cut, copy, paste and select-all.

### Changed

- **Smooth drag-and-drop everywhere.** Reordering your wishlist, your reorder cart, your printers, your camera wall and your storage racks now slides the other items aside to make room, instead of showing a plain line. Your camera streams keep playing while you rearrange.
- **Buy buttons show the shop.** A filament's buy button now reads the actual shop (e.g. "amazon.fr", "atome3d.com") instead of a plain "Buy", so you can see where a link goes before you click.
- **Your stats come to life.** The counters at the top now roll up from zero when the app opens and animate when they change, and the stock weight shows two decimals.
- **Removing a wishlist item now needs a press-and-hold**, so a stray click can't drop a filament by accident.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where your custom friend order was lost as soon as you opened the Friends panel.
- Fixed an issue where the Lists view stayed on screen when switching to Printers or Storage.
- Fixed an issue where a filament could get stuck in your "To order" cart after you un-favorited it — and it now repairs affected accounts automatically.
- Fixed a filament's custom picture not updating in the Favorites grid and table.
- Fixed the stock value not refreshing live when you set a price or switched between pre-tax and tax-included prices.
