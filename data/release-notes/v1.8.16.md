### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the spool detail side panel flashed when something else changed in your inventory — editing a different spool from the mobile app or another device tore down and rebuilt the whole panel, flashing the product photo and every icon. The panel now updates only when the displayed spool's visible fields actually change.
