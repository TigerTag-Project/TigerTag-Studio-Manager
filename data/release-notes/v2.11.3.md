# Tiger Studio Manager v2.11.3

The final round of twin-tag spool cleanup, plus a tidier auto-store.

## Fixed

- The two linked NFC tags of one spool now always agree on the **remaining weight** (and the box), so the stock value reads the same whichever tag the app happens to show — and matches what a friend sees on your inventory.
- Two genuinely **different** spools that had been wrongly linked together as one are now separated back into two, each with its own details and value.
- Clicking **Auto-store** now updates the spool's location on the spot, without the details panel jumping back to the top.

## Changed

- The **Auto-store** button in a spool's details is now a clear sparkle icon that lights up — along with the whole location card — when you hover it, so you can see exactly where the spool will go. The "not stored" label is shorter and reads cleanly in every language.
