### Added

- **Auto-update controls.** A new Updates section in Settings lets you turn automatic update downloads on or off, with a "Check for updates now" button.
- **Auto storage and auto unstorage.** New toggles that automatically place newly added spools into racks and free slots as spools are emptied.
- **Friends quick-access in the sidebar.** Your friends now appear as flat rows under the Friends button; click one to view their inventory.

### Changed

- **Rebuilt Settings panel** — a flat layout with clean sections (Updates / Data / Tools / About) replacing the old card-in-card design.
- The four headline stats (Spools / Stock / TigerTag / TigerTag+) moved from the sidebar to the top of the main pane.
- Storage wording clarified: a free slot reads "Free", and a depleted spool reads "Empty".
- The spool detail panel now shows the spool's storage location (rack and slot), with an Auto-assign button for spools not yet placed.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a person's initials could be hard to read on a light avatar colour; the text now switches to dark automatically.
