### Added

- **3D printer management as a first-class feature.** A new Printers view shows all your printers across the supported brands in a drag-and-drop grid, each card with the printer's photo, model and an online/offline indicator. A side card slides in with the printer's details, and an "Add a printer" flow walks you from a brand picker to a model picker with thumbnails. Every field in the side card is editable on click.
- **Snapmaker: live integration.** Connect to a Snapmaker printer over your local network and see a full-width camera feed, a live print job card (thumbnail, filename, percentage, elapsed time, progress bar, state and layer counter), temperature pills per extruder and bed, and a 4-slot filament grid you can edit inline (vendor, material, colour and sub-type).
