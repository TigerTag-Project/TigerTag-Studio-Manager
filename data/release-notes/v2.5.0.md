# Tiger Studio Manager v2.5.0

The reorder list becomes a proper shopping cart, filament pricing shows up across the app, and the Add-product screen gets a cleaner layout.

### Added

- **A real "To order" cart.** Your reorder list now has two shelves — an active cart for what you need now, and a "saved for later" shelf. Move a spool between them with a click, or drag it. You can also drag items to reorder the list however you like.
- **Stock Value.** A new header card shows how much your current stock is worth, in your currency.
- **Price column.** The inventory table now has a Price column you can sort, showing each spool's price.
- **Buy me a coffee.** If Tiger Studio is useful to you, there's now a friendly way to support the project.

### Changed

- Loving a filament (❤) now keeps at least one in stock, so it automatically shows up in your cart when you run out.
- The Add-product screen now shows Type, Diameter, Weight and Unit up front, and its dropdowns match the rest of the app.
- "RFID" is now "NFC" everywhere in the app — the more accurate name for the technology.
- The device indicators at the top now clearly read "TigerPOD" and "TigerScale", with matching hover tooltips.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where picking a bicolor or tricolor filament didn't update the Aspect 2 field on screen.
- Fixed the Add-product screen opening on the wrong default material.
- Fixed removing a filament from the reorder list wiping its favorite, price and other saved details — it's now safely set aside instead.
- Fixed the app becoming invisible after closing its window on macOS.
