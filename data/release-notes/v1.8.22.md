### Added

- **Custom profile picture.** The coloured-circle avatar everywhere in Studio now shows your uploaded photo when you set one. In the edit-account flow, hover the avatar to reveal an edit pen, then choose Change avatar or Remove avatar. Picking a file opens an "Edit image" window with a circular preview where you can zoom (1×–3×), rotate by 90° and drag to pan until the framing is right, then Apply. Photos are saved as compact JPEG; transparent memojis and illustrations keep their transparency so the avatar's gradient bleeds through. Removing the photo reverts to the colour circle with your initials. Your avatar is visible to your friends and to anyone previewing your friend code before sending a request.
