### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where encoding a custom or third-party spool reset it on the next scan. After writing a tag for such a spool and reading it again, the spool reverted to the generic cardboard container and its weight changed back to the value stored on the chip. The app now keeps the container and weight you set when you re-read an encoded tag.
