### Added

- **RFID chip reuse.** Added three chip actions to a spool's toolbox: **Erase** reinitialises the chip to a blank TigerTag (a TigerTag+ becomes a plain reusable TigerTag), **Recycle to NFC** wipes it back to a generic NFC tag, and **Restore TigerTag+** rewrites a backed-up TigerTag+ to its original state. Each action is restricted to the exact chip on the reader, verifies what it wrote, and requires a press-and-hold to confirm.
- **Snapmaker U1 firmware helper.** For the community Paxx firmware: the download button always points to the latest release, the printer's settings show whether it is up to date, and a notification named after the printer appears when a newer firmware is available. Clicking it jumps straight to that printer.
- **Notification sounds.** A chime plays when a notification actually arrives — a friend request, an accepted request, or a firmware update — but not for the history loaded when the app opens. Printer notifications now show the brand logo, the way a friend's notification shows their avatar.
- **Per-action tooltips in the toolbox.** Each action in a spool's toolbox now has an ⓘ that, on hover, describes in plain words what it does.
- **"Backed up" badge.** A green shield marks each TigerTag+ whose signature is safely backed up, on grid cards, thumbnails and the Storage view.
- **On-chip product ID.** A TigerTag+ Details panel now shows the product ID stored on the chip, linked to its product page.

### Changed

- Rack hover tooltips now show the remaining-filament bar in red / orange / green, matching the rest of the app.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the twin and backup icons did not appear on a freshly-scanned TigerTag+ until the card was closed and reopened.
- Fixed an issue where toolbox actions that need a chip on the reader did not appear or disappear live as the chip was placed or removed while the panel stayed open.
- Corrected the wording of "Remove from inventory" to make clear it is a permanent delete and that the physical chip keeps its data (erase or recycle it to reuse).
