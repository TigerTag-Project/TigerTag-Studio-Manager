## Added

- **Correct a container's empty weight yourself.** The app ships manufacturer weights for every spool container, but yours may not match. Open "Choose a container", tap the pencil next to a weight, and calibrate it with your own scale. The modal shows the container, walks you through what to put on the scale, and compares the catalogue figure with yours before you save. Your correction is kept on your account and used for every spool in that container — the app's own catalogue is never altered.
- **When you save a calibration, you choose what happens to the spools you already own** — update them all with the new weight, or leave them as they are.
- **The "How to measure" guide adapts to the container.** A Masterspool keeps its cardboard core, so both go on the scale; every other type is just the empty spool.

## Changed

- **The chipless tier is now called TigerData** (previously TigerCloud) everywhere you can read it — the badge on a spool, the counter at the top of the window, the filter, and the encode screen. Nothing changes for your spools: it is a name, not a setting.
- **The three tier badges were redesigned** as one family — TigerData, TigerTag and TigerTag+ now read as a ladder in white, silver and gold, and are legible on every background. TigerTag was previously a grey pill on a grey card, effectively invisible.
- **The "Stock value" counter now says whether it is shown excluding or including tax**, so the figure is no longer ambiguous.
- **The inventory's "Protocol" filter is now called "Type"**, which is what it actually filters.
- **Opening "Add product" no longer dims the inventory behind it**, so you can keep reading it while you fill the form.

## Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a custom link added to a filament was not saved and disappeared as soon as you closed the form.
- Fixed an issue where the pencil on the custom-colour swatch became invisible when a white or very pale colour was selected.
- Fixed an issue where the − and + buttons of a number field did not fill their box, leaving a partial highlight when hovered.
