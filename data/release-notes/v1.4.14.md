### Added

- **Multi-colour picker when adding a product.** The colour picker now has a Mono / Dual / Tri / Rainbow selector: tap a colour slot to switch to it, then pick its colour. The colour circle updates in real time — solid for Mono, a hard split for Dual, sectors for Tri, and a smooth gradient for Rainbow.

### Changed

- The Type quick-filter in the inventory toolbar now filters by tag version (TigerTag / TigerTag+ / TigerCloud / TigerTag Init) instead of filament product type.
- The search bar and all quick-filters now clear automatically when you switch accounts or enter and leave a friend's inventory.
