### Changed

- **Bambu Lab camera is smoother and more responsive** on RTSP models (X1C, X1E, P2S, H2x…). The live view now runs at 30 fps instead of 5 and starts almost instantly, with no several-second delay before the first image.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the Bambu Lab camera got stuck on a black screen or spinner on A1, A1 Mini, P1P and P1S. It now reconnects on its own after a printer reboot, a Wi-Fi drop or a slow start, and gives up within a few seconds when the camera port is blocked or unreachable.
- Fixed an issue where the Bambu Lab camera kept trying to open even after you turned the LAN camera off from the printer's own screen.
- Fixed an issue where setting an Anycubic nozzle or bed temperature closed the input on every status update; the field now stays open while you type.
- Fixed an issue where the Anycubic (cloud) job preview thumbnail flickered by reloading on every refresh; it now stays steady.
- Fixed an issue where the printer Table view stayed frozen until you clicked Refresh; each row now updates its own progress, remaining time and online status live. Affects Bambu Lab, Creality, FlashForge and Snapmaker.
