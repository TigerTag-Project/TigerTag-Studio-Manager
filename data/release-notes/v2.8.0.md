# Tiger Studio Manager v2.8.0

Social profiles, follower counts and a clearer public/private status across the app.

### Added

- **Social links on your profile.** Add your Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, X and more from your account settings. The right icon is picked automatically, your links show on your profile, and you can see your friends' links too.
- **Friend and follower counts.** Public profiles now show how many people follow them — useful social proof before you follow along — and your own profile shows your count.
- **Public / Private status on your own profile**, with a one-click button to copy your invite link and share it.
- **Public or Friend badge when viewing someone.** A friend's profile now clearly shows whether their shelf is public or shared just with you, and public profiles offer a button to pass their invite link along.
- **A count badge on the Lists button** so you can see how many lists you have without opening it.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where opening the app and quickly tapping a public account showed it as a regular friend until you left and came back — it now shows the correct public status right away.
- Fixed an issue where a public account's follower count could stay out of date after someone followed it while the owner was offline.
- Fixed an issue where you couldn't reorder your friends while viewing a friend's profile.
