## v2.3.0 — 2026-07-09

Favorites go social: see your friends' favorite filaments — with their price and buy link — and star spools in bulk. Plus a smoother view switcher and a few fixes.

### Added

- **See your friends' favorites.** When you visit a friend, the Favorites grid and table now show the filaments they've starred — their stock, their price, and a clickable buy link. Star one of theirs and its price + link come across to your own favorites. Your private note is never shared.
- **Star several spools at once.** Select a few spools and add (or remove) the heart/star on all of them in one tap.
- **Manage favorites while browsing a friend.** You can select and favorite a friend's materials right from their inventory.
- **New masterspool:** PrintoMax 3D — Grey (195 g).

### Changed

- **Favorites stay reachable in a friend's view** — flip to the Favorites grid or table without leaving; only "To order" is hidden there.
- **A smoother view switcher** — the selection highlight now slides between Grid / Table / Storage instead of snapping.
- **Buy & reorder actions now use a cart icon** instead of the shop logo, so they read as "buy this" at a glance.
- **The "Product info" button now toggles** — click it again to close the card.
- **Click your name in the sidebar** to open your account, just like clicking your avatar.

### Fixed

- Fixed the **in-stock count** counting a twin-chip spool twice (e.g. showing "In stock: 3" for 2 physical spools).
- Fixed a stray **"Detach" button** appearing outside the camera view (including in a friend's inventory).
