### Added

- **Live status and progress in the printer grid and table.** Every connected printer now shows its live state (Idle, Printing, Paused, Preparing, Complete, Error) directly in the grid card and table row without opening the side panel. An active job shows a progress bar with percentage and remaining time, and the truncated filename below it. Remaining time is shown for Bambu Lab, Elegoo and Creality; the filename shows for all brands when printing.
- Clicking a camera on the wall now opens that printer's side panel.

### Changed

- The online badge in printer grid cards now matches the side panel: green when online, amber when connecting, grey when offline.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where printers that connected after the grid first loaded were stuck in the Offline section instead of moving up to Connected.
- Fixed an issue on a fast LAN where a printer's connected status could fail to update.
