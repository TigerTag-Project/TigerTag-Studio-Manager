### Added

- **Detached camera wall.** A new standalone window shows all your online printer cameras at once, opened via the "Detach" button in the Cam view. Supports every camera type (Bambu Lab, Creality, Snapmaker, FlashForge), sharing a single connection per printer so a camera is never opened twice.
- Web-sourced images now show a shimmer placeholder while they load, then fade in.

### Changed

- **Redesigned TigerPOD dialog.** A product helper video replaces the old icon and plays on open, the layout and copy are refreshed across all nine languages, and the feature cards are cleaner. When no reader is connected, "+ Scan" now opens this dialog.
- **Redesigned RFID reader and TigerScale header badges.** The reader badges are now filled pills — red when disconnected, green tint when connected, filled green when a chip is present — and TigerScale has a matching text badge with clear connected / disconnected / unpaired states.
- The colour-edit dialog's edit pencil is always visible, and picks a readable dark or light icon depending on the swatch colour.
- The TigerTag+ product preview now shows the full label (Brand · Series · Name · Weight · Refill) after you check a product ID, with a larger thumbnail.
- Mini-dashboard stat chips now show the proper TigerTag / TigerTag+ / TigerCloud badges instead of plain text.
- The Product ID help dialog and the "Set Color & TD Value" dialog got minor wording and behaviour polish.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a Bambu Lab printer's live data briefly flashed to zero while it reconnected, and where it could be wrongly shown as "idle" during reconnection.
- Fixed an issue where a Bambu Lab printer's status change during connection could rebuild the whole printer grid and swallow a click; opening a printer card from the grid or table is now reliable.

### Removed

- Removed the old Pod Scan side panel, which no longer had any way to open it.
