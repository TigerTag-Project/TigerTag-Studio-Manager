### Added

- **Locked storage slots now have two clear states.** Locking an empty slot marks it as unusable — it gets a grey hatched look and is removed from the rack's available capacity. Locking a filled slot pins the material in place — it keeps the spool's colour with an amber lock badge and is protected from moving and from "Clear all", without changing the slot count.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the Bambu Lab camera showed micro-freezes; frames are now smoothed to remove the stutter bursts on the RTSP range (P2S, H2C, X1) and the JPEG models.
