### Added

- **Anycubic cloud mode now works everywhere, including macOS and Linux.** Adding a cloud printer used to need a Windows-only workaround; now you click Sign in to Anycubic Cloud, log in on Anycubic's own page in a pop-up, and your cloud printers appear. Once added, a cloud printer shows live status, print progress and layers, nozzle and bed temperatures, the ACE filament slots, and — while printing — a preview thumbnail of the actual job. Your password is never seen by the app (you sign in on Anycubic's page) and only the session token is kept.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a cloud-connected Anycubic printer wrongly showed "Idle" while auto-levelling before the first layer; it now shows "Preparing" until printing begins.

> ℹ️ The live camera is not available over the cloud — Anycubic gated their video service behind a newer slicer.
