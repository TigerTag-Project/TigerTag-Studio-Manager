- Fixed an issue where a failed Google sign-in left you stuck on an inert "Use your passkey" screen. When browser sign-in fails, the app now shows a clear message — "Google sign-in via browser failed — please try again or use email/password" — and keeps you on the login form to retry or switch to email/password.
