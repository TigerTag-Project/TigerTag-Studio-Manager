### Added

- **Camera wall size controls.** Each camera on the wall now has a compact ½× size, so four compact cameras fit in the space of one wide one. The card header floats over the feed and appears on hover, so a camera's height is set purely by its 16:9 picture.

### Changed

- Resizing or reordering cameras on the wall no longer restarts the video; streams keep playing through size changes and drag-and-drop reordering.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue on Windows 10 where the app crashed silently at launch on machines with IPv6 disabled; the app now always starts.
- Fixed Creality connection stability so the app no longer opens a connection to an empty address and backs off cleanly from printers that repeatedly fail to answer.
