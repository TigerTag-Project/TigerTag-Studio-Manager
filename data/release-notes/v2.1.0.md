## v2.1.0 — 2026-07-09

A big update built around **Products & Favorites** — keep track of the filaments you care about (stock target, where to buy them, price) even when there isn't a spool of them in your inventory right now. Plus bulk editing, Bambu print previews, and email verification at sign-up.

### Added

- **Favorites & Products.** Star a filament to keep it on a long-lived list — it stays even after you use the last gram. For each one you can save a **minimum stock**, a **buy link**, a **price**, a note, tags and SKU/EAN. A dedicated **Favorites** area (next to Inventory) shows them as a grid, a table, or a **"To order" shopping list**.
- **"To order" shopping list.** A tidy cart of everything you're short on: how many to order, the price per spool and the line totals, with a running **Payment** summary (subtotal, tax, total).
- **Track your stock.** Set a minimum for a product and Tiger Studio Manager warns you — an alert and a notification — the moment your stock drops below it, so you never run out mid-project.
- **Prices your way.** Pick your country (VAT rate + currency) once, and choose whether you type prices **with or without tax** — the app shows both correctly everywhere.
- **Edit many at once.** In the Products table, tick several filaments and set a **price**, add **tags**, or remove them **together**.
- **Bambu print previews.** The model on the bed now shows in the Printers table and the Bambu side card, just like the other brands.
- **Bulk tags.** Tick several spools (or printers) and tag them all in one go.
- **Email verification.** Signing up with an email + password now sends a confirmation email before your first sign-in.

### Changed

- **A click opens the product deck first.** In the Inventory grid or table, clicking a filament now opens its grouped view first — even for a single spool — then you pick the exact spool to see its details.
- **Prices follow your tax setting** in the "To order" list and its Payment summary.
- **Print previews stick around** after a job finishes, across every brand, until the printer goes idle.
- **Cleaner, native feel** — text no longer gets accidentally highlighted as you click around.

### Fixed

- Fixed a faint **shadow that lingered along the right edge** of the window.
- Fixed **padlocks showing on a friend's Storage** view where they didn't belong.
- Fixed the **"Auto-organize"** label not translating.
- Fixed **info tips getting clipped** in the Storage view.
- Fixed a bogus **"Door closed"** badge on open-frame FlashForge printers.
- Fixed the **"Ends at"** column staying blank for Snapmaker and FlashForge prints.
- Fixed the multi-select **Delete** button so its hold-to-confirm is clearly visible.
- Fixed launching straight into a **Printers view** showing the wrong filters.
