### Changed

- **Storage view overhaul.** A new stats bar shows total racks, filled-vs-total slots, empty and locked counts, and a clickable "Not Stored" tile; the Empty and Locked tiles double as filters. Each rack has an inline header and a menu for Edit, Auto-fill, Lock/Unlock all, Clear all and Delete, with a press-and-hold on destructive actions. While dragging a spool, valid slots pulse and the target slot pops with an orange ring, swap targets show a ⇄ glyph, and slots animate as they land or clear. Racks pack tightly into the available width and reflow on resize, and filled slots have a rich hover tooltip (brand, material, colour, coordinate, weight bar).
