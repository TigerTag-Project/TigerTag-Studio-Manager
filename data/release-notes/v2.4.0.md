## v2.4.0 — 2026-07-10

A tidy new product card for filaments you don't have in stock, a filter by finish/aspect, one-tap SKU & EAN copying, cleaner dropdowns — and friends' favorites now stay perfectly in sync with them.

### Added

- **A product card for out-of-stock favorites.** Click a favorite you don't currently have on a shelf (including a friend's) and a clean read-only card opens with the colours, aspect, print settings, price, buy link, documents and video — everything but the spool-specific bits. Star or love it right there to add it to your own list.
- **Filter by aspect.** A new "All aspects" selector next to the material filter lets you narrow down by finish (Matte, Silk, Carbon…) or colour arrangement (Bicolor, Tricolor, Rainbow…). It only lists the aspects you actually have.
- **One-tap SKU & EAN copy.** In the "To order" list and the product card, just click a SKU or EAN to copy it — a little "Copied!" pops up. No clutter, no extra buttons.

### Changed

- **Friends' favorites stay in sync.** When you browse a friend, their favorites — with price and buy link — are now read straight from their list, so what you see is always up to date.
- **Cleaner filter dropdowns.** The Brand / Material / Aspect / Version / Tag and Sort menus now match the app's look instead of the plain system menu.
- **Favorites filters show only what's in your favorites** — the brand, material and aspect choices there are scoped to your starred filaments.
- **A smarter product info panel.** Its header opens (and closes) the product card, the layout is tidier, and the stock line reads simply as "Stock: 2 / 5".
- **Editing a SKU or EAN** now uses a single pencil that turns into a green check — press Enter or click it to save.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where importing a friend's favorite could create a duplicate and lose the "added from" credit.
- Fixed favorite prices sometimes showing without tax when your account is set to tax-inclusive.
- Fixed the material filter in Favorites listing finish-specific entries (e.g. "PLA Basic") instead of just the material ("PLA").
- Fixed a favorite you no longer stock not opening its editable info card.
