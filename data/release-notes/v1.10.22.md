### Changed

- **Refreshed "empty inventory" welcome screen.** It now shows a product mockup of Studio and the TigerTag RFID app, with the headline above the image, and highlights that you can scan and manage both your inventory and your 3D printers from your phone.
- **One beta install card for everyone.** The beta card now mirrors the App Store & Google Play card and carries a single universal link that sends iPhone users to TestFlight and Android users to the Android beta automatically.
- Change your avatar straight from the header: hover your own avatar and click the edit badge to pick, crop and upload a new photo, or use the new "Add/Change photo" entry in the account menu.
- QR codes are no longer clickable, since they are meant to be scanned with your phone camera.
- The camera "Detach" button is now hidden when viewing a friend.
