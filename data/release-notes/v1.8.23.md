### Changed

- All coloured-circle avatars across the app (sidebar, header chip, dropdowns, modals, friend chips, friends panel, friend-view header) now render through a single pipeline, so the same avatar looks identical everywhere.
- Friend chips now use a proper gradient, matching the look of your own avatar.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where your avatar flickered through several states on every cold start (empty circle, then a wrong letter from your email, then your real initials, then your photo) and the sidebar friends list showed up empty until the server replied. The app now paints your cached avatar, name initials and friends instantly and only repaints if the server returns something genuinely different.
- Fixed an issue where a Google placeholder photo overwrote your uploaded avatar on every sign-in; a one-time cleanup runs for affected users the next time the app opens.
- Fixed an issue where the "+" sign-in badge showed next to your initials while you were already signed in.
- Fixed an issue on Windows 10 where the avatar upload's crop window failed to open, silently cancelling the upload. macOS and Windows 11 were never affected.
