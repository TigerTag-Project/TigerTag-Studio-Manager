### Added

- **Creality printers now show up in the network scan.** Add printer → Creality → Scan network discovers Creality machines on your LAN (K-series, K2, and current-gen Enders running Klipper such as the Ender-3 V4) and adds them in one click, just like Snapmaker and FlashForge. There's also a manual "Enter IP address" path and an "Add by IP" shortcut. Verified live against an Ender-3 V4.

### Changed

- Adding a Creality printer no longer requires a username or password; those fields are now optional, since most Creality printers connect without authentication.
- Viewing a friend's inventory now hides the "+ Scan" and "Add" buttons, since those actions can't apply to someone else's collection.
