### Added

- **Google sign-in with Touch ID and passkeys.** Sign-in now opens your system browser, so Touch ID, passkeys and hardware security keys work natively, and the app is brought back to the front automatically after you sign in.
- **Twin pairs on the TigerScale card.** Two tags that reference each other now show as a single physical spool on the scale's card.
- A welcome greeting shows when viewing your own inventory, and a clear read-only badge shows when previewing a friend's; the sidebar avatar doubles as a one-click way to return to your own inventory.

### Changed

- Legacy spool IDs are migrated to the new format automatically and safely in the background, with a consent prompt showing the spool count and estimated duration, and a progress screen during the sweep.
