### Added

- **TigerCloud — the third tier.** Create a filament in your inventory without owning an RFID chip. A TigerCloud spool is a fully digital entry with the same fields, display and friend-sharing as a chip-backed spool. When you later program a physical chip, the entry is promoted automatically — its twin link, rack assignment, weight history and friend access all follow, with no manual effort. TigerCloud appears as a purple tier alongside TigerTag+ (orange) and TigerTag (grey), across the table, grid cards and detail panel.
- **Full colour picker when adding a product.** Pick from an anthracite preset sheet or open a custom colour picker with a saturation/value area, hue slider, hex input and live preview.
