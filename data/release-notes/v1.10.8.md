### Added

- **Anycubic cloud-mode camera.** Printers connected in cloud mode now show their live camera in the side panel, not just LAN-connected printers. Thanks to [@ennisj](https://github.com/ennisj) (PR #3).

### Changed

- Adding a printer now opens its panel automatically instead of leaving you on the list.
- Closing a printer also closes its Settings form, so no leftover panel floats on the side.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where, with a spool card, a printer's Settings and a printer panel all open side by side, the spool card painted over the Settings panel's close tab; the tab is now always reachable.
