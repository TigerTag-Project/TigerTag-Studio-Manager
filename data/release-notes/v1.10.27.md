### Added

- **Community links.** A badge and a notification invite you to join the Discord, star the project on GitHub, and browse the free 3D files on MakerWorld. Each shows once and never nags again after you click through.

### Changed

- **Refreshed printer control panel.** Dedicated icons for the nozzle, bed and chamber temperatures, fan, homing and disable-motors, plus compact Step and Speed selectors, all sharing one consistent colour.
- Creality printers can now set their print speed from the control panel, like the other brands.
- **Clearer notifications.** Each notification shows an icon, a title and a one-line message, and the whole notification is clickable.
- Tidier sidebar community buttons (GitHub / 3D Files / Discord): same size, square when the sidebar is collapsed, with larger logos.
- "Spools not stored" now lists empty spools last, so the rolls you still need to rack stay at the top.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the sidebar briefly showed placeholder text as the app loaded; labels now show their final wording on the first frame.
- Fixed an issue where friend-request and app notifications were cut off; they now wrap to show the full text.
- Fixed an issue where an Elegoo printer's chamber temperature showed the bed icon.
