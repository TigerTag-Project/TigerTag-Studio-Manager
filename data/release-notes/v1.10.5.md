### Added

- **Anycubic Kobra X: live camera now works (LAN).** The Kobra X's camera is now supported over the local network, reusing the same video pipeline as the Kobra 3 V2; it was previously left off because the Kobra X advertises its stream differently. Thanks to [@ennisj](https://github.com/ennisj) (PR #2).
