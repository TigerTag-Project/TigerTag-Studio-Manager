### Added

- **"What's New" screen.** After an update, a floating window shows what's new in plain language — move it, resize it, and browse the notes of any past version from the dropdown. Reopen it anytime from Settings → About → "What's New".

### Changed

- **Cleaner, emoji-free interface.** Emojis across the app are now crisp SVG icons, or removed where they were purely decorative, for a more consistent look.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where a TigerCloud spool and a TigerTag (Maker) spool of the same filament did not collapse into the same group; they now group together.
- Fixed an issue where the "Set Color & TD Value" dialog could not be moved; you can now drag it by its header.
