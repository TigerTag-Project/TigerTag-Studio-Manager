## Fixed
- **"Switch account" now opens the level-2 sub-menu instead of closing the dropdown.** The sub-menu navigation re-renders the dropdown, which detached the clicked button before the document-level outside-click handler ran — so it treated the click as "outside" and closed everything. Fixed with `stopPropagation` on the level-1↔2 navigation. Also hid the scrollbar on the companion friends-list card. `renderer/inventory.js`, `css/20-friends.css`
- **Scanning a spool while viewing a friend returns you to your own inventory AND opens the spool's detail card.** A pod/RFID scan always acts on the active account, so both scan entry points (`onRfid`, `onRfidTagScanned`) now exit friend view (`switchBackToOwnView`) the moment a tag is read. The scanned spool is queued (`_scanOpenUid`) and opened in the detail side-card by `_consumeScanOpen` — called from the scan handlers AND from the inventory snapshot, so it reliably opens once the spool lands in the own inventory, even across the async view switch / Firestore write. `renderer/inventory.js`
- **"Blocked users" section refreshes when the Friends panel opens.** `openFriends` now calls `loadBlacklist()` so the blocked-users list (with its Unblock buttons) is always current, not only from login time. `renderer/inventory.js`
- **Buttons no longer turn white-on-white on hover (root cause fixed once and for all).** The global `button:hover { background: var(--surface-2) }` was overriding the fill of every custom-coloured (orange) button on hover, leaving white text on a light background — so each orange button had to manually redeclare its `:hover` background or it broke. Replaced it with `button:hover { filter: brightness(.95) }`, a slight darken that's visible on light, dark AND coloured backgrounds without ever overriding the button's own colour. `.ghost`/`.primary` opt out (they keep their explicit hover backgrounds). New coloured buttons now stay readable on hover for free. `css/70-detail-misc.css`
- **Auto-organize waits for all required data before acting (readiness barrier).** Auto-store/auto-unstore now refuse to run until the active account's racks have actually been delivered (`_racksLoadedFor === activeAccountId`), instead of firing on the first cached inventory snapshot while racks are still stale/empty. The barrier is armed on every account teardown (`handleSignedIn`, `unsubscribeRacks`) and lifted by the racks snapshot, which then re-runs both passes so spools scanned before the racks loaded still get placed and depleted spools in unlocked slots still get freed. This is the structural root-cause fix; the orphan-pool restriction and `state.racks` reset remain as defense-in-depth. `renderer/inventory.js`
- **Auto-organize never reassigns an "orphan" spool — it only displays it.** `autoFillEmptySlots` now restricts its pool to genuinely-unranked spools (`rackId == null`). A spool whose rackId points to a rack not in `state.racks` (rack snapshot still loading, e.g. mid account switch, or a rack deleted without its cascade) still SHOWS in the not-stored pile but its location is never rewritten. This makes the destructive path fail-safe by design — the root structural fix for the cross-account rack corruption (complements the `state.racks` reset on account switch). `renderer/inventory.js`
